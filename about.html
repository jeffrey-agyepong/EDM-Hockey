Fred Vossr

Owner

Upon finishing his Pro Hockey Career in Switzerland, Fred journeyed to Canada to start the Swiss International Sports and Education Center (SISEC) in 2005. Based in Chestermere to start, Fred was one of the founding 5 figures of the Canadian Sports School Hockey League (CSSHL). This league is now a pinnacle operation in Canada, with just over 80 teams competing.

Darcy Hodge

General Manager

Before Darcy became a member of the International Hockey Academy, he was an Academy father. He has been with the program for 6 years now. In the academy, he has been a skills coach, a head coach and now is the program's general manager. He is an High Performance 1 Certified coach. Before coming onto IHA, he owned the largest Skills Academy in Sherwood Park, working with upwards of 350 kids a summer. He developed position specific training programs, that has helped a number of kids move onto the NHL, AHL, European Pro, NCAA, CIS and all levels of Junior hockey in Canada

Jason Abramoff

U17 Prep Head Coach

Jason has been with the International Hockey Academy for 5 years now. He has held the roles of assistant and head coach. This year, Jason continue as our HEAD COACH of the U17 PREP team and as our Director of Player Development.
